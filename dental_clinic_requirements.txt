DENTAL CLINIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
REQUIREMENTS AND FEATURES DOCUMENT
=====================================

1. PATIENT MANAGEMENT
---------------------
1.1 Patient Registration
    - Personal information (name, DOB, address, contact details)
    - Emergency contact information
    - Insurance information
    - Medical history and allergies
    - Consent forms and document management

1.2 Patient Records
    - Complete dental history
    - Treatment records
    - X-rays and imaging storage
    - Progress notes
    - Prescriptions history
    - Patient photo/identification

1.3 Patient Search and Filter
    - Search by name, phone, ID
    - Filter by appointment status, treatment type
    - Quick access to frequent patients

2. APPOINTMENT MANAGEMENT
-------------------------
2.1 Scheduling
    - Calendar view (day/week/month)
    - Drag-and-drop appointment booking
    - Multiple dentist/chair management
    - Recurring appointments
    - Appointment duration customization
    - Color-coded appointment types

2.2 Appointment Features
    - Appointment reminders (SMS/Email/Push)
    - Waitlist management
    - No-show tracking
    - Cancellation and rescheduling
    - Online appointment booking portal

3. TREATMENT MANAGEMENT
-----------------------
3.1 Treatment Planning
    - Treatment plan creation
    - Cost estimation
    - Treatment progress tracking
    - Before/after photo documentation
    - Treatment approval workflow

3.2 Clinical Features
    - Dental charting (odontogram)
    - Treatment codes (CDT codes)
    - Procedure notes
    - Treatment history
    - Diagnosis recording

4. BILLING AND FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
------------------------------------
4.1 Invoicing
    - Invoice generation
    - Payment processing (cash, card, online)
    - Multiple payment methods
    - Partial payment tracking
    - Payment receipts

4.2 Financial Reporting
    - Daily/monthly revenue reports
    - Outstanding payments tracking
    - Expense management
    - Profit/loss statements
    - Tax reporting

4.3 Insurance Management
    - Insurance verification
    - Claims processing
    - Pre-authorization management
    - Insurance payment tracking
    - Multiple insurance plan support

5. STAFF MANAGEMENT
-------------------
5.1 Staff Records
    - Dentist profiles
    - Support staff information
    - Credentials and certifications
    - Working schedules
    - Role-based access control

5.2 Staff Scheduling
    - Shift management
    - Availability tracking
    - Leave management
    - Performance tracking

6. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
-----------------------
6.1 Supplies Tracking
    - Dental supplies inventory
    - Medication inventory
    - Equipment tracking
    - Stock level alerts
    - Reorder management

6.2 Suppliers Management
    - Supplier database
    - Purchase orders
    - Vendor pricing
    - Delivery tracking
